The AI Sphere

Beyond detection — a suite of LLM-powered features turning security data into clear, actionable guidance.

Ask AI: Explainable Security

Transforms the detection engine from a black box into a transparent system. Plain-language explanations let users make informed decisions rather than blindly trusting automated verdicts.

AI Device Remediation

Analyses device configurations and recommends jargon-free actions to reduce attack surface — disabling risky settings and aligning your device with current security best practices.

Fake News Detection

A multi-phase AI fact-checking engine applying LLM models in sequence to evaluate URL reliability, identify fabricated claims, and detect coordinated misinformation — returning a structured factuality score.
